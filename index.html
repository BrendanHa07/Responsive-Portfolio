About Me

Hi there! My name is Brendan Ha and I welcome you to my page. A little bit about me is that I graduated from UCLA in 2011 with a degree in biology. I currently work at a microbiological lab that focuses on food safety. We do a variety of food safety testing for the greater Southern California area. I chose to get into coding because I felt as though a career change was in order. Technology and computers have always interested me and I believe that this is the right time to dive into this exciting and innovating field.

Some of the things that make me tick include basketball, attending concerts, and craft beer. There is nothing better than going to either a Lakers game or concert and downing a cold, frosty microbrew!
